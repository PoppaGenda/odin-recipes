French Fries

Description

The french fries is a recipe that involves potatoes.These fries is a go to snack for everyone.It is considered a very popular fast food and is consumed by millions of people

Ingredients

  • 2 cup potatoes
  • salt
  • oil

Steps

  1. Soak the potatoes of it's starch
  2. Chop the potatoes into long rectangular pieces
  3. Add salt to the chopped potatoes
  4. Finally,fry the potatoes in oil
  5. Remove from pan using a spatula.Let it rest,the dish is prepared
