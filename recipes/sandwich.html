Sandwich

Description

Sandwich is a dish which you can easily make.You can prepare it anytime,whether it is before going for office or school.A large variety of sandwiches exist.

Anybody can create a sandwich irrespective of their age.It is one of the easiest dishes that one can prepare

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Onions
  • Capsicum
  • Tomatoes
  • Baby corn
  • Mayyonaise
  • Tomato ketchup
  • Cheese slice

Steps

  1. Cut the vegetable into small pieces.
  2. Take a loaf of bread and add mayyonaise and tomato ketchup to it
  3. Add a slice of Cheese onto that loaf
  4. Now,Add the chopped vegetables to it
  5. Add another loaf of bread over it and grill it for 2 mins
  6. Cut the sandwich into four slices .Finally it is ready to be eaten.
