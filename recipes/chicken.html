Crispy Chicken

Description

The crispy fried chicken is a finger licking recipe that involves tender chicken pieces to be deep fried.The balanced spices along with a coating of bread crumbs bring a crispy texture to the chicken.

The recipe might seem like a fried chicken,but I can assure you crispy chicken is healthier than your normal day to day chicken

Ingredients

  • 10 chicken legs
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • oil
  • bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degree
  2. Add oil to a pan.Roll chicken pieces in bread crumbs and melted butter
  3. Bake the dish for 1 hour
